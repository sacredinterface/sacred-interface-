The Seven Houses

Each House holds a sacred resonance. Together they form the full prism of Ilarian creation.

  • House of Light – Illumination, awareness, and origin.
  • House of Flame – Passion, will, and purification.
  • House of Breath – Spirit, voice, and motion.
  • House of Stone – Structure, memory, and endurance.
  • House of Water – Emotion, reflection, and adaptability.
  • House of Seed – Growth, life, and renewal.
  • House of Shadow – Mystery, depth, and transformation.
